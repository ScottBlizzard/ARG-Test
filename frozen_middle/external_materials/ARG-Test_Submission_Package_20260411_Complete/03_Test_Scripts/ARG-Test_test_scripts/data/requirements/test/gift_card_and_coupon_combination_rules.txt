Requirement ID: gift_card_and_coupon_combination_rules
Domain: payment promotion

Rules:
1. Gift cards may be used together with at most one coupon.
2. Coupon discount is applied to merchandise subtotal (excluding shipping and tax) before applying gift card balance.
3. Gift card balance is applied after coupon and can cover shipping and tax.
4. Coupons cannot discount gift card products.
5. The final payable amount must never be negative.
6. Invalid inputs: multiple coupons, expired coupon, gift card code invalid.
