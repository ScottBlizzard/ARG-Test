Requirement ID: free_shipping_threshold_by_member_tier
Domain: e-commerce shipping

Rules:
1. member_tier is one of Guest, Silver, or Gold.
2. For local or domestic orders, Standard shipping becomes free when subtotal reaches the tier threshold.
3. Thresholds are: Guest 120, Silver 100, Gold 80.
4. Express shipping is never free.
5. International orders never get free shipping.
