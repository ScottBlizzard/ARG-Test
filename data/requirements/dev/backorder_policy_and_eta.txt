Requirement ID: backorder_policy_and_eta
Domain: e-commerce inventory

Rules:
1. If available_stock is 0 and backorder_allowed is true, the item may be purchased as backorder.
2. Backorder requires estimated_ship_days between 1 and 60.
3. If backorder_allowed is false and stock is 0, checkout is rejected.
4. Negative stock is invalid.
