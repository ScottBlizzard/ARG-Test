Requirement ID: shipping_method_eligibility_by_weight
Domain: e-commerce shipping

Rules:
1. Supported methods are Standard, Express, and Freight.
2. weight must be non-negative.
3. Standard is available for weight up to 20 kg.
4. Express is available for weight up to 5 kg and only when hazardous is false.
5. Freight is required when weight is above 20 kg.
