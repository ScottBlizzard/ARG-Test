Requirement ID: gift_card_partial_redemption
Domain: e-commerce payment

Rules:
1. A gift card has balance (non-negative).
2. A user may redeem redeem_amount greater than 0.
3. redeem_amount must not exceed gift card balance.
4. redeem_amount must not exceed order_total.
5. On success, new_balance equals balance minus redeem_amount, and the order payable amount reduces by redeem_amount.
