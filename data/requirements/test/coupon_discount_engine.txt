Requirement ID: coupon_discount_engine
Domain: promotion engine

Rules:
1. Only one coupon may be applied to an order.
2. SAVE10 gives 10 percent off when subtotal is at least 50.
3. SAVE20 gives 20 percent off when subtotal is at least 100 and the customer is a premium member.
4. FREESHIP sets shipping fee to 0 when subtotal is at least 30.
5. Expired coupons are invalid.
6. SAVE20 cannot be used when the order already contains sale items.
7. A valid coupon changes only the allowed target and must never make subtotal negative.
