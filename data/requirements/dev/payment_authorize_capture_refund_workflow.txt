Requirement ID: payment_authorize_capture_refund_workflow
Domain: e-commerce payment workflow

Rules:
1. Card payments start as Authorized.
2. Authorized may transition to Captured exactly once.
3. Authorized may transition to Voided; void is not allowed after capture.
4. Refunds are allowed only after Captured.
5. Multiple refunds may occur, but total_refunded must not exceed captured_amount.
