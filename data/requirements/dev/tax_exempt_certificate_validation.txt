Requirement ID: tax_exempt_certificate_validation
Domain: e-commerce tax

Rules:
1. Tax exemption is supported only for US orders.
2. certificate_id must match pattern EX-XXXXXXXX where X is a digit.
3. certificate_expiry_date must be on or after order_date.
4. If a valid certificate is present for a US order, tax_rate becomes 0.
5. Otherwise tax is calculated normally.
