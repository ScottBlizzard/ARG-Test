Requirement ID: refund_amount_calculation_rules
Domain: e-commerce refunds

Rules:
1. refund_base equals paid_amount minus non_refundable_fees.
2. If item_opened is true, apply restocking_fee_rate 0.10 capped at 50.
3. refund_amount equals refund_base minus restocking_fee, and must be between 0 and paid_amount.
4. If refund_amount would be negative, it becomes 0.
