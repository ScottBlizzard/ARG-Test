Requirement ID: checkout_address_postcode_format
Domain: e-commerce checkout

Rules:
1. postcode validation depends on country.
2. US: either 5 digits (e.g., 12345) or 9 digits with dash (e.g., 12345-6789).
3. CN: exactly 6 digits.
4. UK: 5 to 8 alphanumeric characters, may contain a single space.
5. For other countries, postcode must be non-empty and at most 12 characters.
