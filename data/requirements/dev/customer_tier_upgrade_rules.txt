Requirement ID: customer_tier_upgrade_rules
Domain: e-commerce loyalty

Rules:
1. Tiers are Bronze, Silver, and Gold.
2. Tier is determined by rolling_12m_spend.
3. If rolling_12m_spend is at least 2000, tier is Gold.
4. Else if rolling_12m_spend is at least 500, tier is Silver.
5. Otherwise tier is Bronze.
