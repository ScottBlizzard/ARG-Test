Requirement ID: preorder_deposit_and_balance_payment
Domain: e-commerce preorder

Rules:
1. A preorder defines deposit_percent between 10 and 30.
2. deposit_amount equals order_total * deposit_percent and is due immediately.
3. The remaining balance is due before ship_deadline_date.
4. If deposit is not paid, the preorder is cancelled.
5. If balance is not paid by the deadline, the preorder is cancelled.
