Requirement ID: exchange_request_workflow_state
Domain: e-commerce exchange workflow

Rules:
1. An exchange request starts in Requested.
2. Support may approve Requested to Approved or reject Requested to Rejected.
3. After Approved, customer ships item back: Approved to ShippedBack.
4. Warehouse receives item: ShippedBack to Received.
5. Replacement shipment: Received to ReplacementShipped.
6. Completion: ReplacementShipped to Completed.
7. Customer may cancel only while Requested.
