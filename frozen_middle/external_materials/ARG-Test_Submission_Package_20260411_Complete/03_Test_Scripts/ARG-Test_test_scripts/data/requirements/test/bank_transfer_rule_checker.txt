Requirement ID: bank_transfer_rule_checker
Domain: online banking

Rules:
1. transfer amount must be between 1 and 5000 inclusive for standard users.
2. transfer amount must be between 1 and 20000 inclusive for verified users.
3. daily transferred total must not exceed 10000 for standard users and 50000 for verified users.
4. Transfers above 2000 require a valid one-time password.
5. Transfers to a blacklisted account must be rejected.
6. Transfers to the same source account must be rejected.
7. Transfers that exceed available balance must be rejected.
