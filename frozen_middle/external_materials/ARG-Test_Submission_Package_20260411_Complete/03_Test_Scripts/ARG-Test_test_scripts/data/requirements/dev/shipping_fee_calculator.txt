Requirement ID: shipping_fee_calculator
Domain: e-commerce checkout

Rules:
1. destination zone is one of local, domestic, or international.
2. order subtotal and package weight must be non-negative.
3. For standard shipping, local fees are 5 for weight up to 1 kg, 8 for weight above 1 kg and up to 5 kg, and 12 for weight above 5 kg.
4. For standard shipping, domestic fees are 8 for weight up to 1 kg, 12 for weight above 1 kg and up to 5 kg, and 18 for weight above 5 kg.
5. For standard shipping, international fees are 20 for weight up to 1 kg, 35 for weight above 1 kg and up to 5 kg, and 50 for weight above 5 kg.
6. Express shipping adds a surcharge of 10 for local or domestic and 25 for international.
7. Express shipping is unavailable when weight is above 20 kg.
8. Standard shipping is free for local or domestic orders when subtotal is at least 100.
