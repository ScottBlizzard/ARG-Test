Requirement ID: checkout_promo_stack_and_priority
Domain: checkout promotion

Rules:
1. At most one coupon code may be applied to an order.
2. Supported coupons:
   - PERCENT10: 10% off merchandise subtotal when subtotal >= 100.
   - FIXED20: 20 off merchandise subtotal when subtotal >= 200.
   - SHIPFREE: shipping fee becomes 0 when subtotal >= 80.
3. Coupons never apply to tax; percent/fixed coupons apply only to merchandise subtotal (excluding shipping and tax).
4. Loyalty points may be redeemed after coupon discount; points redemption is capped by the remaining payable amount (cannot make payable negative).
5. Expired/unknown coupon codes are invalid.
6. If multiple coupons are provided, the checkout must reject the promotion input.
