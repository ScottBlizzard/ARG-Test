Requirement ID: fraud_risk_scoring_rules
Domain: e-commerce fraud checks

Rules:
1. Risk score starts at 0.
2. Add 30 if ip_country differs from shipping_country.
3. Add 30 if order_total is above 1000.
4. Add 20 if account_age_days is less than 7.
5. Add 20 if failed_payment_attempts is at least 2.
6. If risk score is at least 70, the order is flagged for manual review.
