Requirement ID: bundle_discount_eligibility_rules
Domain: promotion pricing rules

Rules:
1. Bundle A applies when the cart contains at least 1 laptop and at least 1 eligible mouse.
2. Bundle B applies when the cart contains at least 1 console and exactly 2 eligible games.
3. Sale items are not eligible for bundle discounts.
4. Only one bundle discount may apply to the same cart line.
5. If multiple bundle discounts are eligible, apply the one with the highest absolute discount amount.
6. Bundle A discount is 120 when the laptop unit price is at least 4000; otherwise 80.
7. Bundle B discount is 150.
8. Illegal: stack a bundle discount with coupon code BUNDLE-EXTRA.
