Requirement ID: cart_merge_on_login
Domain: e-commerce cart

Rules:
1. When a guest user logs in, the guest cart is merged into the user's existing cart.
2. For the same sku, quantities are summed; if the sum exceeds 99, it is capped at 99.
3. If the merged quantity exceeds available_stock, it is reduced to available_stock and a warning is returned.
4. If the guest cart is empty, the user's cart remains unchanged.
