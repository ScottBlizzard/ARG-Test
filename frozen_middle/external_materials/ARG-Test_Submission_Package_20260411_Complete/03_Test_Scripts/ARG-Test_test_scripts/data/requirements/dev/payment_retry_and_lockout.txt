Requirement ID: payment_retry_and_lockout
Domain: e-commerce payment workflow

Rules:
1. A payment attempt starts in Ready state.
2. After 3 consecutive failed payment attempts within 5 minutes, the payment is Locked for 10 minutes.
3. A successful payment in Ready resets the consecutive failure counter to 0.
4. In Locked, any attempt returns "payment locked" without contacting the provider.
5. After 10 minutes, the payment returns to Ready and the counter resets.
