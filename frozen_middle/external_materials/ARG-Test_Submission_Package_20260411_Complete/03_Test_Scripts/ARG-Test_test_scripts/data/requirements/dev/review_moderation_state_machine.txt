Requirement ID: review_moderation_state_machine
Domain: e-commerce reviews workflow

Rules:
1. A new review starts in Submitted.
2. Submitted transitions to Pending when queued for moderation.
3. A moderator may approve Pending to Approved or reject Pending to Rejected.
4. A moderator may hide an Approved review, moving it to Hidden.
5. Hidden reviews remain hidden until explicitly restored to Approved.
