Requirement ID: gift_order_recipient_contact_validation
Domain: gift order delivery validation

Rules:
1. A gift order requires recipient_name.
2. delivery_channel must be EMAIL or SMS.
3. When delivery_channel = EMAIL, recipient_email is required and must be a valid email format with length 5 to 100.
4. When delivery_channel = SMS, recipient_phone is required and must be in E.164 format (+ followed by 8 to 15 digits).
5. scheduled_send_days_ahead is optional, but if provided it must be between 0 and 365 inclusive.
6. gift_message is optional with maximum length 200.
7. Illegal: both recipient_email and recipient_phone missing for the selected delivery channel.
